# Week 9: Investigating mobile platforms, devices and hardware

Learning Objective

By the end of this week, students will:

  • Know where to find, how to use and why to use mobile platform guidelines
  • Compare guidelines across various platforms
  • Understand the implications of hardware and devices on UI design
